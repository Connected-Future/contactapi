How it works

Send email and any JSON.

Whatever fields you send get saved as-is. Send the same email again and it updates in place instead of making a duplicate.

POST /v1/contacts secret or publishable

ContactAPI
Keys & MCP

Two keys and an MCP server.

Pick the right key for where it runs, and let AI clients manage contacts through the MCP server.

ck_secret_… For your backend. Full access to every endpoint.
ck_pub_… For the browser. Can only create contacts, locked to your domains.
/mcp Point Claude or Cursor at the URL and let them manage your contacts.
